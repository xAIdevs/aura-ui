8 Layers of Protection

📸
Photo Verification
All profile photos are verified using AI facial recognition to prevent fake profiles and catfishing. Verified users get a ✓ badge.
🔍
Background Check
Optional but encouraged — run a background check through our partner Checkr to verify identity and screen for registered sex offenders.
🕶
Incognito Mode
Browse profiles privately with AURA Pro. Control who can see that you've visited their profile, on your terms.
🚫
Block & Report
Block anyone instantly from their profile or a conversation. Reports are reviewed by our human moderation team within 2 hours.
📍
Safe Meeting Guide
Built-in guide for safe first meetings — public venue suggestions, share your plans feature, and check-in reminders sent to your emergency contact.
🆘
Emergency Contacts
Add up to 3 trusted contacts. They receive your live location automatically when you activate the safety check-in for a date.
🔒
Safe Message Filter
AI automatically detects and filters threatening, harassing, or inappropriate messages before they reach your inbox — with full context preserved for reporting.
👁
Location Privacy
Your exact location is never shown. We only display distance in miles/km. Your neighborhood and home address are never stored or shared.
How to Report

Reporting is
fast and easy

1
Tap the ⋯ menu
On any profile or in a conversation, tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner.
2
Select "Report" or "Block"
Choose the appropriate action. Blocking immediately prevents any contact, reporting sends a review request.
3
Choose a reason
Select the category that best describes the issue. The more detail you add, the faster our team can act.
4
We investigate
Our moderation team reviews your report within 2 hours and takes action — from warning to permanent ban.

Community Guidelines

✅

Be Authentic

Use real photos and honest information. Catfishing and fake profiles are permanently banned.

🤝

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with kindness. Harassment, hate speech, and discrimination are zero-tolerance offenses.

🔒

Protect Privacy

Never share other users' photos, contact info, or private messages outside of AURA.

🚫

No Solicitation

No spam, scams, promotional content, or solicitation of any kind. Violations result in immediate removal.

🔞

Adults Only

AURA is strictly for users 18+. Attempting to connect with minors results in permanent ban and is reported to authorities.

💬

Honest Intentions

Be upfront about what you're looking for. AURA celebrates all relationship types — just be honest about yours.

Contact Safety Team

Have a safety concern that doesn't fit the in-app report form? Reach out directly.

We respond within 4 hours. For emergencies, call 911.
