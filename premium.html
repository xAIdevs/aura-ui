Yes, annual plans auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date. You'll receive a reminder email 7 days in advance.

Absolutely. You can upgrade from AURA+ to Pro, or downgrade, at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
