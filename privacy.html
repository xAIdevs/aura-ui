Section 02

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service — Creating your account, showing your profile to potential matches, enabling messaging
  • AI Compatibility Matching — Running our AURA BRAIN™ algorithms to generate compatibility scores and match recommendations
  • Personalization — Customizing your feed, suggestions, and AI coaching based on your behavior and preferences
  • Safety & Trust — Detecting fake accounts, fraud, prohibited content, and violations of our Terms
  • Communications — Sending transactional messages (OTP, account alerts), product updates (with your consent), and support responses
  • Analytics & Improvement — Understanding how users interact with AURA to improve features and fix bugs
  • Legal Compliance — Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and lawful requests

We never sell your personal data to advertisers. AURA is funded by subscriptions, not advertising.

Section 03

AI & Compatibility Engine

The AURA BRAIN™ is our proprietary AI system that powers compatibility matching. Here is exactly what it does and does not do:

  • It analyzes your profile, stated preferences, and interaction patterns (which profiles you engage with, response times, conversation quality) to generate a Compatibility Score
  • It infers Personality Vectors — behavioral patterns across 47 dimensions — to predict interpersonal chemistry
  • It does not read the content of your private messages for matching purposes
  • It does not make decisions about who you can match with based on protected characteristics (race, religion, ethnicity, nationality)
  • AI-generated compatibility scores are probabilistic suggestions, not deterministic outcomes

🤖 Human oversight: All AURA AI systems are subject to regular audits for bias and fairness. You can request a review of any AI decision affecting your account by contacting privacy@aura.app.

You may opt out of behavioral-pattern-based matching in Settings → Privacy → AI Preferences. Opting out means your matches will be based on stated preferences only.

Section 04

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only in the following circumstances:

  • With other AURA users — Your public profile (name, age, photos, bio, interests) is shown to users you appear in their discovery feed. Your precise location is never shared; only your approximate city/distance.
  • With service providers — We use vetted third-party vendors for cloud hosting (AWS), SMS delivery, payment processing (Stripe), and analytics. These vendors process data only under our instructions and strict confidentiality obligations.
  • For legal reasons — We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.
  • Business transfers — If AURA is acquired or merges, user data may be transferred to the new entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections. You will be notified in advance.

When you report another user, your report details (but not your identity) may be shared internally to investigate the matter.

Section 05

Data Storage & Security

AURA takes data security seriously. Our measures include:

  • Encryption in transit — All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest — All stored personal data is encrypted using AES-256
  • Access controls — Strict role-based access; employees can only access data necessary for their function
  • Penetration testing — Regular third-party security audits and bug bounty program
  • Photo hashing — We use perceptual hashing to detect known CSAM and non-consensual intimate images

Our primary data centers are located in India (Mumbai), with backup infrastructure in Singapore. If you are an EU/UK user, your data is processed under Standard Contractual Clauses.

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

Section 07

Cookies & Tracking

AURA's web experience uses the following types of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential cookies — Required for authentication, security, and core functionality. Cannot be disabled.
  • Performance cookies — Collect anonymized data about how users interact with the app to help us improve it (e.g., page load times, error rates). You can opt out.
  • Analytics cookies — Help us understand aggregate usage patterns using tools like anonymized event tracking. You can opt out.

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking technologies. We do not participate in any advertising networks or real-time bidding systems.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time in Settings → Privacy → Cookies.

Section 08

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

  • Active account data — Retained until you delete your account
  • Messages — Stored for 3 years after the last message in a conversation, then permanently deleted
  • Account data after deletion — Deleted within 30 days, except where required for legal compliance
  • Safety-related logs — Retained for up to 2 years to investigate reported violations
  • Backup copies — May persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days after deletion
  • Financial records — Retained for 7 years as required by tax law

When you delete your account, your profile is immediately hidden from other users. Full deletion from our systems is completed within 30 days.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

AURA is strictly intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

We use a combination of age verification at registration, AI-based age estimation on profile photos, and community reporting to enforce this restriction. If we discover or are notified that an account belongs to someone under 18, we will:

  • Immediately suspend the account
  • Delete all associated personal data
  • Notify the reporter (without disclosing the minor's information)

If you believe a minor is using AURA, please report them immediately using the in-app report function or by emailing safety@aura.app.

Section 10

International Data Transfers

AURA operates globally. If you are located outside India, your data may be transferred to and processed in India, where our primary infrastructure is located.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we transfer data under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, which provide appropriate safeguards for your personal data.

For users in California, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) / CPRA. California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information (though we do not sell personal data).

For users in India, we comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page

Your continued use of AURA after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes. If you disagree with any changes, you should stop using the Service and delete your account before the effective date.
